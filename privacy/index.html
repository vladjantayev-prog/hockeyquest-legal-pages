Privacy Policy

HockeyQuest

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HockeyQuest ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

1. Who We Are

HockeyQuest is operated by Vladislav Jantayev. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at hockeyquestapp@gmail.com.

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may process the following information:

  • Workout and drill progress, scores, streaks, and completed sessions
  • Motion, body-position, or training-session data needed for drill tracking and feedback
  • Camera input processed to detect body movement, stance, reactions, or exercise repetitions
  • Language preference and app settings
  • Subscription and purchase status
  • Basic usage and technical information needed for the app to function

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide hockey drills, stance tracking, reaction drills, and training feedback
  • Show scores, streaks, workout history, and other progress features
  • Support subscriptions and paid features
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services to provide certain functionality. These may include:

  • RevenueCat — for subscription and purchase management
  • Apple — for In-App Purchases, subscriptions, and App Store services
  • Analytics or error-monitoring tools, if enabled in the app in the future

These services may process data only as needed to provide app functionality.

5. Camera and Motion Data

HockeyQuest uses camera-based movement tracking to detect body position, reactions, exercise repetitions, and drill performance.

Video is used to provide app functionality. Please avoid capturing sensitive personal information or third parties in camera view unless necessary.

6. Subscriptions and Payments

Payments and subscription handling are processed by Apple through In-App Purchases and may be managed through RevenueCat. We do not receive your full payment card details.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the app, provide requested features, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

8. Your Choices

You may choose not to grant camera access, but some app features may not work properly without it.

9. Children's Privacy

HockeyQuest is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hockeyquestapp@gmail.com.
