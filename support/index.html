HockeyQuest Support

If you need help with HockeyQuest, we’re here for you.

Contact us at hockeyquestapp@gmail.com.

Common Topics

  • Subscription or payment issues
  • Restore purchase not working
  • Camera or drill tracking not working correctly
  • Workout bugs or unexpected behavior
  • Questions about privacy or data

When Contacting Support

Please include:

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if relevant

We’ll do our best to respond as quickly as possible.
